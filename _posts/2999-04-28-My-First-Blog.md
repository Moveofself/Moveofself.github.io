## Summary

学习是一个自我提高的过程，伴随人的一生。不管在生活还是工作中，我们都要把学习知识当成一种生活态度、一种工作责任、一种精神追求。

Learning is a process of self-improvement, accompanied by a person's life。 Whether in life or in work, we should regard learning knowledge as a life attitude, a work responsibility and a spiritual pursuit。
